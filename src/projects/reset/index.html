Description

ReSet, Recovery after Stress toolkit, is a web applicatin directed towards 6-14 year olds who have gone through traumatic events. the tool itself was a part of a program to find the best way to help children connect with their feelings and cope with them during a time when people were stuck in their homes during the Covid-19 pandemic. We also built a way for therapists to track prgression and connect online with their patients.
